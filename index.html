Location Intelligence

Cakemix Labs builds AI-based analytics tools for location. We sell a platform to optimize retail locations, routing, scheduling, and supply chain configuration—allowing our customers to outmaneuver and overcome their competitors.

Cakemix continuously models staged-growth strategies against competitor actions so our customers will be ready for any situation. Know competitors' moves before they do, and be ready to exploit their weaknesses—locally, globally, and in the short and long term.

Competitive Advantage

The Cakemix platform equips our customers with the equivalent capabilities of the world's best, most-talented industrial engineering teams, coupled with an AI built specifically to learn how to best achieve customer objectives.
